# 👋 Hi, I'm Indulekha K. P.

I am a PhD Scholar in Electrical Engineering at IIT Madras, working on wireless communication, V2X networks, and machine learning-based resource allocation.

My research interests include vehicular networks, NR-V2X, multi-agent reinforcement learning, integrated sensing and communication, and cross-layer optimization for next-generation wireless systems.

- Resource allocation and scheduling for ISAC based systems
- Integrated sensing, communication, and computation in vehicular systems
- Reinforcement Learning for Scheduling and network optimization
